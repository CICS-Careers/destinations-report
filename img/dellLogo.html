[Brand Guidelines] Logo

Logo

Our Dell Technologies logo is how the world recognizes us and we are consistent in how we use it.

Primary Use

Prioritize the use of the blue, horizontal Dell Technologies logo to build brand consistency and recognition. Use the stacked logo when it visually works within the design and space allocated.

Alternate Variations

When the Dell Blue logo isn’t suited to a particular application, prioritize the white and black one. The gray version should be reserved for limited applications.
